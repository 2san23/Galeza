Discover Egypt tours visiting Pyramids of Giza

See the Sphinx and Giza Plateau highlights, then dive into Cairo’s museums and markets

Egypt tours visiting the Pyramids of Giza place one of the world’s most famous ancient sites at the heart of the journey. From the Giza Plateau, take in close-up views of the Pyramids and the Sphinx, then continue into Cairo for key collections at the Grand Egyptian Museum. Many guided tours combine these headline sights with other classic Egyptian experiences, from temple-filled Nile Valley cities to time by the Red Sea. Trip styles range from group departures to private touring, with itineraries that balance guided sightseeing with free time. Some trips are multi-country, visiting places such as Jordan, Turkey, United Arab Emirates, and more.
