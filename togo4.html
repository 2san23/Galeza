Discover Egypt tours visiting Aswan

Sail the Nile, explore riverside temples, and soak up Aswan’s easygoing charm

Aswan is a standout stop on many Egypt tours, known for its relaxed riverside setting and easy access to remarkable ancient sites. Expect a mix of guided tours and well planned travel that links Aswan with other key places in Egypt, often including time on the Nile. Highlights can range from temple visits reached by boat, to time browsing colorful souks, and even traditional felucca sailing. Some itineraries also include experiences such as a camel ride to the Monastery of St Simeon or visits that offer insight into local life. Multi country tours are also available, with some itineraries visiting Egypt and Jordan.
