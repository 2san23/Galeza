IN CAIRO

Nestled within the bustling metropolis of Cairo, you can find wellness centers and spas with all the pampering and wellness amenities that you need. Luxury spas in many of the city’s five-star hotels offer everything from Thai massages to a specialty facial in clean, comfortable surroundings with procedures administered by professional standard practitioners. Exercise studios and fitness centers offer a broad assortment of classes, including yoga, Pilates, spinning, CrossFit, functional training, and much more. The city has also become home to young, health-conscious small businesses offering organic products that include essential oils, natural skincare, and aromatherapy products.
