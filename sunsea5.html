KITTING & WINDSURFING

Strong winds, shallow lagoons, and flat waters offer superb conditions for kiting and windsurfing on the Red Sea coastlines. Ras Sedr, Dahab, and Al Gouna are all kiting and windsurfing hotspots, ranked as some of the top spots globally, each with its own distinct atmosphere and amenities. Head to Dahab for a relaxed cozy beach town vibe, Al Gouna for a bigger resort town with endless entertainment options, and Ras Sedr for an easily accessible weekend getaway from Cairo. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced professional, kite and windsurfing centers are available to cater to every level of surfer with knowledgeable trainers ready to help visitors learn.
