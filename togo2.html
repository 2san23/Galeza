Discover Luxor tours in Egypt

Step into ancient history through Luxor’s temples, tombs and Nile-side scenery

Luxor is a highlight of many Egypt tours, offering some of the country’s most celebrated ancient sites in one compact area. Guided tours in Luxor often focus on the great monuments of the east bank, including the vast Temple of Karnak, then continue to the west bank for the Valley of the Kings and other landmark tombs. Some itineraries also include time for a night visit to Luxor Temple, or add nearby sights such as Dendera. Luxor frequently features on trips that combine Cairo with Nile cruising and time in Aswan, with some tours also including a Red Sea stay in Hurghada. Multi-country options are also available, visiting Egypt alongside Jordan, Israel, or Turkey.
